About me

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a junior student of School of Physics and Astronomy(SPA) in Sun Yat-sen university(SYSU).

In my sophomore year, I followed my undergraduate supervisor for scientific research training, and my research direction was the hydrodynamic evolution of protoplanetary disks based on the ATHENA++ code.

In my junior year, my research turned into the hydrodynamic evolution of the circumbinary disk. I'm trying to understand the interactions among planets, central binary and the disk.
